Hi, I'm Ioana. Nice to meet you.

I was born in a beautiful town in the middle of Romania and found my way around the world to where I am now. I'm lucky to be here, surrounded by family and my amazing dog, Princess Luna — who inspires so much of what I do. That's how LunaBelle Games was born.

I've loved video games my whole life — playing them, getting lost in them, appreciating the ones that make you feel something. What started as a personal fun project quickly turned into a very ambitious vision.

If you'd told me a few years ago that I would be here today, I would have laughed in your face and said — me? Making games? Nah. But here we are. I managed to publish two cute and cozy games, and who knows what comes next. I don't know. Nobody knows. But I do know that I want to dream big and go for it. Life is too short to be afraid to try.

Thanks for stopping by — and if you tried my games and liked them, that's awesome and makes me very happy.
